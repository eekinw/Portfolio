More About Me

Hi! Ee Kin here, thanks for visiting my website.

Currently a Business Operations Analyst in a wonderful tech startup in Kuala Lumpur called SUPA.

Took the plunge to start learning how to code in late 2022 just because I thought it was cool - and here I am, loving the learning process every single day.

Fell in love with Frontend Development as I'm fascinated with all the moving parts in a website/application and curious to know more about the people behind these screens. Understanding the users better drives me to provide a better user experience for them.

Outside work, I am an avid language learner (well, just one). Learned Japanese during the pandemic because I was frustrated I couldn't read Japanese kanji when they look similar to Mandarin (my mother tongue). 3 years had gone by, and I've since passed JLPT N2 in 2022. Currently looking to be fluent in business-level Japanese!

Anyways, hope to share more exciting (and random) stories in the future. Stay tuned for updates!
